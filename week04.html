I do most of the things we will do in this class on a daily basis for my job. Not saying I am any good at this kind of stuff, but that I am familiar with it and have some experience. While completing the assignments this week, I started to notice a bad habit of mine. I use many resources for my job because those are the resources my boss prefers. After looking into so many different options I realized there are a lot of really good options for different things that I would have never considered. I tend to stick with what I know because I spent so much time learning how to use it already, but I think I need to be more open to other resources that might be more helpful to me depending on the project. So I think what I learned the most this week is there is so many options to choose from, and there likely is more than one option that will fit what you are looking for.
